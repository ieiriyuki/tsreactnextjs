## StyledButton

StyledButton is a custom component. Color of button is changed by `variant`.

- [Primary](#primary)
- [Success](#success)
- [Transparent](#transparent)

### Primary

```tsx
<StyledButton variant="primary">Primary</StyledButton>
```

